Buenos Aires, 23 May (NA) --
Today, the Foreign Ministry said that control operations carried out by the corvette Spiro against a Korean-flagged [as received] ship fishing illegally in Argentine waters were carried out "in accordance with international law and in coordination with the Foreign Ministry."
The crew was transferred to the Chin Chuan Hsing, which was sailing nearby and approached to rescue the crew of the sinking ship.
