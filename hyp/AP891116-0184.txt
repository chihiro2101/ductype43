Tornadoes were reported in New Jersey, Delaware, Virginia and New York, although the National Weather Service was unable to confirm them.
The same storm system caused tornadoes in seven states in the South and Midwest on Wednesday, and was blamed for 17 deaths in Huntsville, Ala.
High temperature records fell in advance of the storm and sharply colder weather lurked behind it.
